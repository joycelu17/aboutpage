Founding Story

Earlier this summer we got started on our first product, AggieWalk, a reliable and accurate course reviewer which includes data for both professors and classes. Our current team of 5 has been working hard on the website and hope to have the initial launch as the fall quarter opens up.

The team wants to ensure that all of our products have pristine design and is really addressing user needs. With a vision to have the most trusted source of crowd-source academic information at Davis, our team sent out surveys to students and started to implement important features we observed to be of importance. Ultimately, we wish to make this just one of many products to use technology and improve life at Davis.
